SIMPLE CAKE RECIPE

This recipe is a great recipe to make a simple choclate cake. It uses ingredients that can be found in most kitchen cupboards. You just need to gather all the ingredients, put them into a big bowl and mix them with a blender or mixer! It can also be used to make cupcakes too, if you adjust the cooking times as mentioned below. Then you can decorate your cake however you'd like to.

Equipment Needed:

  1. Cake or muffin tray
  2. Mixing Bowl
  3. Mixer
  4. Cooling rack
  5. Fork
  6. Scales
  7. Smaller bowls for ingredients

Ingregients for Cake:

  1. 2 Cups Flour - GF Flour is OK, too!
  2. 2 Eggs - Duck Eggs work well also!
  3. 1 Cup Milk - any kind you like!
  4. 1 tsp Vanilla Extract - I like Vanilla from Mexico, but I grew up with the stuff.
  5. 1/2 Cups Sugar
  6. 8 Tbsp Butter
  7. 1 1/2 Tsp Baking Powder

Ingredients for Icing:

  1. 2 Cups Confectioner's Sugar
  2. 1/2 Cup Softened Butter
  3. 1 1/2 Tsp Vanilla Extract
  4. 2 Tbsp Milk - again, any kind you like!
  5. **Optional: 3 drops of food coloring**

Utensils:

While much of the equipment has already been listed, I feel the following list is more accurately described as Utensils. Honestly, it comes down to semantics, but that is how I feel it should be.

  1. Measuring Cups
  2. Measuring Spoons
  3. Wire Whisk or Hand Mixer
  4. Pam or Equivalent (I use butter or coconut oil in the pan)
  5. Spatula - to make sure the full portion of cake batter makes it into the oven.

Mixing & Baking Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Combine wet ingredients (milk, eggs, vanilla, softened butter) in a bowl and mix well.
  3. Add dry ingredients, mixing well until smooth
  4. Grease bakind dish with Pam or alternative
  5. Pour cake batter into baking dish
  6. Bake for 35 to 40 minutes, or until golden brown
  7. Remove from oven once toothpick placed in the middle comes out clean
  8. Allow to cool before icing
